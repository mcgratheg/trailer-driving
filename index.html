Controls

W/S : Forward/Reverse
A/D : Left/Right (Fine turning)
Z/X : Left/Right (Large turning)
C   : Centre Wheels


Challenges

Challenge One:
Try to drive the car around the two obstacles
without touching them, and then try to reverse the
car into the green parking space!


Challenge Two:
Try to drive the car around the three obstacles
without touching them, and then try to reverse the
car into the green parking space!
